## Frameworks/Principles
**Free Space/Fluid/Stillness/Motion**:
- Free Space: Minimalist grids (Goya math).
- Fluid: Bubble ripples, vine growth (CSS particles/Godot shaders).
- Stillness: Dial pause moments.
- Motion: SFX spins, hero dances.

**Typography/Textography**:
- Primary: Hyperpop sans (rounded, playful).
- Secondary: Runes monospace for stats.
- Gradients: Rainbow unity to black inverse villains.
