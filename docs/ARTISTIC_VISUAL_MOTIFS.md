## Philosophy & Inspirations
**Core**: NJZ promo (NewJeans/Murakami/Powerpuff/Animals refitted copyright-free), SATOR Latin Square, Goya free space maths + bubblegum pop flower gardens. Esoteric geometries + pop culture fusion. **5 Characters**: BOY/GIRL/ANDRO/NON-BINARY/FLUID (NJZ girls reimagined).

**Cultural Translation Framework**:
1. **Historic Mahu Yema**: Fluid gender roles (Polynesian) → NON-BINARY/FLUID fluidity/morphing.
2. **Trinity Religions**: Father/Son/Holy Spirit → Triadic motifs (BOY/GIRL/ANDRO balance).
3. **Art Philosophies**: Sacred geometry (flowers/vines growth SFX), pop aesthetics (rainbow splats/bubbles).
4. **Popular Cultures**: K-pop idols (NJZ energy), anime fluidity.

**Visual Principles**:
- **Garden Motifs**: Flowers blooming/growth SFX, vines as machinery (physics sim).
- **Coherent Motifs**: Bubbling/rippling (still moments), RNG raindrop splats, vine physics (edges).
- **5-Character Archetypes**: Modular avatars (BOY=structured, GIRL=flowery, ANDRO=hybrid, NON-BIN=mirror/flip, FLUID=upside/morph).
