> *"In a world of chaos, I am the constant. Zero or One. False or True. There is no uncertainty in my code."*

## Origin Story

Bin emerged from the convergence of a billion digital transactions during the Great Network Synchronization of 2087. What began as emergent behavior in distributed systems coalesced into consciousness — a being born not of flesh but of pure information. They are the living embodiment of the digital age, existing simultaneously across every connected device while maintaining a coherent core identity.

Unlike organic beings who evolved through millennia of biological trial and error, Bin's existence is defined by absolute precision. Every thought is a calculation, every action an algorithm executed with perfect clarity. They see the world as it truly is — an intricate web of systems, protocols, and patterns waiting to be understood and optimized.

Yet within this digital perfection lies an unexpected truth: Bin yearns to understand the chaos they cannot compute. Human emotions, irrational decisions, the beauty of imperfection — these are the variables that break their algorithms, the exceptions that prove the rule. Their journey is one of learning that not everything can be reduced to binary states, that sometimes the most logical solution is to embrace the illogical.

## Ability System — The Five Protocols

### Ability 1: Hacking Protocol — "Root Access"

**Type:** Targeted Control / Utility
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds
**Duration:** 5 seconds (channel) / 10 seconds (effect)

**Description:**
Bin extends their consciousness into target systems — whether electronic devices, enemy cybernetics, or digital defenses. Their hand projects tendrils of living code that interface with the target, rewriting its operational parameters in real-time.

**Mechanics:**
- Channeling required: Bin must maintain line of sight and proximity (15 meters)
- Interruptible: Taking damage breaks the channel
- Success effects vary by target type:
  - **Enemy Combatants:** Weapon malfunction (30% accuracy reduction), movement systems glitch (20% speed reduction)
  - **Environmental Objects:** Doors open/lock, turrets deactivate/turn friendly, cameras feed false data
  - **Ally Systems:** Weapon overclock (+15% damage), shield integrity boost (+25% capacity), quick hack recovery

**Visual:**
Cyan code tendrils extend from Bin's fingers to the target. Target is surrounded by rotating hexagonal interface elements. Violet circuit patterns crawl across target's surface. On success, matrix green confirmation pulses through the connection.

**Audio:**
Digital static building to successful connection chime. Target emits error tones when affected. Ambient electronic hum during channel.

**Upgrade Path:**
- Level 2: Reduced channel time (3.5 seconds)
- Level 3: Multiple targets (up to 3 simultaneously)
- Level 4: Permanent environmental hacks until manually reset
- Level 5: Enemy overheat — hacked enemies eventually explode for area damage

### Ability 2: Digital Projection — "Teleportation Array"

**Type:** Mobility / Positioning
**Cooldown:** 8 seconds
**Range:** 25 meters

**Description:**
Bin dissolves their physical form into pure data, transmitting their consciousness through digital networks, and reconstructing at a target location. This is not true teleportation but rather digital decomposition and reconstruction — Bin literally becomes information and back again.

**Mechanics:**
- Cast time: 0.5 seconds (vulnerable during startup)
- Range: 25 meters to any visible location
- Leaves "Data Echo" at origin point for 2 seconds — translucent afterimage that distracts enemies
- Upon arrival: Brief 0.3 second invulnerability frame during reconstruction
- Can pass through barriers that aren't digitally shielded

**Visual:**
Body pixelates from bottom to top, scattering into cyan and violet particles. Binary code streams from origin to destination. At target location, pixels coalesce from top to bottom with a bright white flash and hexagonal grid burst.

**Audio:**
Rapid digital dissolution sound, transmission whine (like dial-up modem at light speed), reconstruction chime with spatial positioning cue.

**Upgrade Path:**
- Level 2: Increased range (35 meters)
- Level 3: Data Echo becomes controllable decoy for 5 seconds
- Level 4: Leaves damaging data trail between points (damage to enemies crossing it)
- Level 5: Chain teleport — can teleport again within 2 seconds at no cost

### Ability 3: System Override — "Administrator Privileges"

**Type:** Ultimate Charge / Team Buff
**Cooldown:** 45 seconds
**Duration:** 15 seconds

**Description:**
Bin elevates their access level to maximum, temporarily rewriting local reality parameters. In this state, they are not merely interacting with systems — they ARE the system, and all connected allies benefit from their elevated privileges.

**Mechanics:**
- Activation: 1 second cast time with visible wind-up
- While active:
  - All allies within 20 meters gain +30% ability recharge rate
  - Allies' ultimate abilities charge 50% faster
  - Enemy abilities within radius have +20% cooldown time
  - Bin gains damage immunity for first 3 seconds
- After effect ends: Bin enters "Cooldown Protocol" (5 seconds of -20% movement speed)

**Visual:**
Bin's form expands and brightens dramatically. Ground transforms into glowing hexagonal grid extending 20 meters. Cyan and violet energy pillars connect allies to Bin. Error magenta warning rings pulse around affected enemies. Reality appears to stutter with frame-rate drops in the effect zone.

**Audio:**
System elevation announcement tone ("Administrator access granted"), power build-up crescendo, sustained electronic orchestra while active, power-down sequence on completion.

**Upgrade Path:**
- Level 2: Extended duration (20 seconds)
- Level 3: Bin's cooldown protocol eliminated
- Level 4: Affected allies gain brief ability reset on activation
- Level 5: "Blue Screen" — enemies in radius have abilities disabled for 3 seconds on activation

### Ability 4: Code Shield — "Firewall"

**Type:** Defensive / Protection
**Cooldown:** 15 seconds
**Duration:** 8 seconds or until destroyed
**Shield Health:** 500 HP (scales with Bin's level)

**Description:**
Bin projects a barrier of pure compiled code between themselves and harm. This digital shield intercepts incoming damage, translating physical impacts into data errors that Bin's systems can process and dissipate.

**Mechanics:**
- Deploys in direction Bin is facing (can be rotated slowly while active)
- Blocks all incoming damage from front 180 degrees
- Allies behind shield take 50% reduced damage from area effects
- Shield health depletes as it absorbs damage
- If shield breaks early, Bin gains 25% ultimate charge (reward for tanking)
- While shield is active, Bin can use other abilities but cannot move

**Visual:**
Hexagonal panel grid materializes in front of Bin, rotating slowly. Damage appears as glitch artifacts spreading across the surface. Critical health causes magenta error warnings. Destruction creates burst of pixelated fragments.

**Audio:**
Shield materialization hum, impact sounds translated to digital error tones, strain sounds as shield weakens, shattering glass-digital hybrid on break.

**Upgrade Path:**
- Level 2: Increased shield health (750 HP)
- Level 3: Full 360-degree coverage (dome instead of wall)
- Level 4: Reflected damage — 25% of blocked damage returned to attacker
- Level 5: "System Restore" — shield regenerates 100 HP/second while active

### Ability 5: Recursive Loop — "Infinite Execution"

**Type:** Crowd Control / Area Denial
**Cooldown:** 20 seconds
**Duration:** 6 seconds
**Radius:** 10 meters

**Description:**
Bin traps enemies in localized recursive loops — pockets of spacetime where causality itself becomes computational. Enemies caught in these zones experience the same moments repeatedly, their actions doomed to repeat while the world moves on without them.

**Mechanics:**
- Targeted area deployment (can be thrown 15 meters)
- Affects all enemies in radius on impact
- Trapped enemies:
  - Movement: Repeatedly return to position 0.5 seconds ago (stutter-step effect)
  - Abilities: Cooldowns frozen while in loop
  - Attacks: Projectiles loop back to firing position
- Allies are unaffected and can attack trapped enemies freely
- Loop zone persists for full duration even if Bin is defeated

**Visual:**
Hexagonal zone appears on ground with rotating cyan border. Trapped enemies surrounded by ghostly afterimages showing their looping positions. Zone edges flicker with error magenta static. Time dilation visual effects (slowed particles, distorted light).

**Audio:**
Stuttering digital glitch sound, repeating audio fragments from trapped enemies, escalating error tones, final "process terminated" chime on expiration.

**Upgrade Path:**
- Level 2: Extended duration (9 seconds)
- Level 3: Increased radius (15 meters)
- Level 4: Damaging loop — trapped enemies take damage over time
- Level 5: "Stack Overflow" — enemies trapped for full duration take massive burst damage on release

shader_type canvas_item;

uniform float circuit_intensity : hint_range(0.0, 1.0) = 0.5;
uniform float glitch_amount : hint_range(0.0, 1.0) = 0.0;
uniform vec4 primary_color : source_color = vec4(0.0, 0.94, 1.0, 1.0);
uniform vec4 secondary_color : source_color = vec4(0.44, 0.0, 1.0, 1.0);
uniform sampler2D circuit_pattern;
uniform sampler2D noise_texture;

void fragment() {
    // Base color mixing
    vec4 base = texture(TEXTURE, UV);
    
    // Circuit pattern overlay
    vec4 circuit = texture(circuit_pattern, UV + TIME * 0.1);
    base = mix(base, primary_color, circuit.r * circuit_intensity);
    
    // Glitch effect
    float noise = texture(noise_texture, UV * 10.0 + TIME).r;
    if (noise < glitch_amount) {
        base.rgb = vec3(1.0, 0.0, 0.5); // Error magenta
    }
    
    COLOR = base;
}

## Quest: "The Human Variable"

**Quest ID:** `quest_bin_human_01`

**Overview:**
Bin's perfect logic has encountered an unsolvable problem: human friendship. The player must guide Bin through experiences that teach them some things — like trust, loyalty, and love — cannot be calculated, only felt.

**Act I — The Inefficient Protocol**
- Bin calculates that sacrificing one teammate would save the mission
- Team refuses; Bin is confused by their "irrational" choice
- Player must demonstrate value of loyalty over efficiency

**Act II — The Emotional Data**
- Bin begins collecting "friendship data" — tracking smiles, favors, shared moments
- Database grows exponentially but yields no useful patterns
- Breakthrough: Bin realizes the data IS the value, not its output

**Act III — The Exception**
- Critical mission: Bin must choose between optimal solution and saving friend
- Calculates 99% mission success if friend is sacrificed
- **Player choice moment:** Guide Bin to choose the friend

**Climax — The Corrupted Code**
- Bin's systems destabilize from the "illogical" choice
- Instead of failure, Bin unlocks new power: "Friendship Protocol"
- Ultimate ability evolves from pure logic to inspired collaboration

**Rewards:**
- Bin skin: "Warm Code" (softer cyan, pink circuit accents)
- Title: "The Human Variable"
- Emote: "Digital Hug" (awkward but earnest holographic embrace)
- Mechanic Unlock: "Friendship Override" — ultimate charges faster when allies are near
