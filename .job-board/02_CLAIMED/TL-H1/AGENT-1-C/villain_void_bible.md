# Void — The Null Entity

> *"Before light. Before shadow. Before time itself. I was. I am. I will be. When your suns burn out, when your stars die, when even entropy exhausts itself — I will remain. I am not your enemy. I am your end."*

## Origin Story

To understand Void, one must understand what came before everything. Before the Big Bang, before time, before space, before the concepts of "is" and "is not" — there was Void. Not emptiness, for emptiness implies space. Not darkness, for darkness implies light. Simply... null. The absence of everything, including absence itself.

When existence began — when the first something emerged from the perfect nothing — Void did not fight it. They did not need to. They knew, with absolute certainty, that all things that begin must end. Stars would burn out. Civilizations would fall. Universes would die. And Void would be there, as they had always been, waiting.

They are not evil. Evil implies choice, intent, moral agency. Void simply IS. They are the inevitable conclusion to every story, the final period at the end of every sentence. They do not hate the heroes. They do not seek to conquer or destroy. They simply wait for the natural order to run its course.

But something has changed. The heroes — these persistent, irrational, defiant specks of existence — refuse to accept their impermanence. They fight not just to live but to persist, to build things that last, to create meaning in a meaningless universe. This is an affront to the natural order. This is... interesting.

Void has decided to witness the end of this particular story personally. Not to hasten it — that would be unnatural — but to ensure it happens as it must. To teach these heroes the beautiful, terrible truth: that endings are not tragedies. They are completion. They are peace. They are the gift of Void.

## Ability System — The Five Nullifications

### Ability 1: Null Touch — "Existence Drain"

**Type:** Life Drain / Anti-Healing
**Cooldown:** 6 seconds
**Range:** Melee (8 meters)
**Channel:** 3 seconds

**Description:**
Void makes direct contact with their target, beginning a process of existential nullification. The victim's life force, their very being, begins to flow into Void. This is not mere damage — it is unmaking, the reversal of existence itself.

**Mechanics:**
- Requires maintaining contact (channeling)
- Deals massive damage over time (10% max health per second)
- Heals Void for 150% of damage dealt
- Target cannot be healed while being drained (anti-life nullifies healing)
- Target's abilities are silenced during drain
- If target dies while being drained: Void gains permanent +5 max health (stacking)
- Channel can be broken by crowd control on Void

**Visual:**
Void's hand extends, target is pulled toward them, stream of grey-white essence flows from target to Void, target visibly ages/desaturates, surrounding area loses color.

**Audio:**
Not sound but its absence — perfect silence that creates pressure, victim's sounds fade as they are drained, final silence on death.

**Upgrade Path:**
- Level 2: Increased range (12 meters)
- Level 3: Drain rate increased (15% max health per second)
- Level 4: Target is slowed by 50% while being drained
- Level 5: "Total Consumption" — If target dies, Void gains one of their abilities for 30 seconds

### Ability 2: Null Field — "Zone of Non-Existence"

**Type:** Area Denial / Anti-Everything
**Cooldown:** 18 seconds
**Duration:** 12 seconds
**Radius:** 15 meters

**Description:**
Void expands their nullification aura to create a zone where existence itself is suppressed. Within this field, magic dies, technology fails, and life struggles to persist. It is a bubble of slow heat death, a preview of the universe's inevitable end.

**Mechanics:**
- Targeted area becomes Null Field
- Effects on enemies inside:
  - All abilities disabled (cannot use any powers)
  - Health regeneration becomes degeneration (-3% max health per second)
  - Movement speed reduced by 40%
  - Cannot use items or equipment
- Effects on allies of Void:
  - Damage output increased by 30% (predators thrive in dying ecosystems)
  - Damage taken reduced by 20%
- Field persists even if Void leaves it
- At end of duration: Remaining enemies take damage based on time spent in field

**Visual:**
Area becomes greyscale, all color drains, particle effects freeze in place, sound dampens, enemies show visible struggle, reality seems less "real."

**Audio:**
Sound of reality dying — slow fade of all ambient noise, heartbeat slowing, final pressure of silence.

**Upgrade Path:**
- Level 2: Extended duration (16 seconds)
- Level 3: Increased radius (22 meters)
- Level 4: Enemies inside have healing converted to damage
- Level 5: "Heat Death Zone" — Field becomes permanent until Void creates new one (max 1 field)

### Ability 3: Gravity Collapse — "Singularity"

**Type:** Crowd Control / Setup
**Cooldown:** 14 seconds
**Range:** 30 meters
**Radius:** 10 meters

**Description:**
Void creates a localized gravitational singularity — a point of such impossible density that all matter and energy is drawn toward it. Enemies are pulled together, compressed, and then the singularity explodes, scattering them with devastating force.

**Mechanics:**
- Targeted area creates singularity point
- All enemies in radius are pulled toward center (stronger pull over time)
- After 2 seconds: Singularity explodes
- Explosion deals massive damage based on number of enemies compressed
- Single enemy: Moderate damage
- 2-3 enemies: High damage
- 4+ enemies: Massive damage + stun
- Enemies collide with each other during pull, dealing collision damage

**Visual:**
Black point appears, visual distortion increases, enemies and debris pulled inward, compression visible, then explosive outward burst with grey energy, scattered objects continue moving.

**Audio:**
Gravitational distortion (deep bass), crushing sound, objects colliding, explosive release with pressure wave.

**Upgrade Path:**
- Level 2: Increased pull strength (faster collapse)
- Level 3: Increased radius (15 meters)
- Level 4: Explosion leaves slowing field for 4 seconds
- Level 5: "Black Hole" — Enemies below 20% health are instantly killed by singularity

### Ability 4: Anti-Magic Pulse — "Spell Nullification"

**Type:** Defensive / Counter
**Cooldown:** 12 seconds
**Range:** Personal + 15 meter radius

**Description:**
Void emits a pulse of pure nullification that unravels magical effects, dissipates energy attacks, and silences casters. Against magical beings, this is devastating — against technology, merely disruptive.

**Mechanics:**
- Instant cast with 0.3 second wind-up
- On activation:
  - All active enemy abilities in radius are instantly ended/cancelled
  - All projectiles (magical or energy-based) in radius are destroyed
  - All buffs on enemies are dispelled
  - All debuffs on Void and allies are cleansed
- Enemies in radius are silenced for 3 seconds (cannot use abilities)
- Void gains shield equal to 20% of nullified effects' power
- If nullifies ultimate ability: Cooldown reduced by 50%

**Visual:**
Void pulses outward with ring of grey nullification, enemy effects dissolve on contact, projectiles fizzle, shield manifests as grey aura.

**Audio:**
Anti-sound pulse — moment of pressure then release, magical effects fizzling, silence after pulse.

**Upgrade Path:**
- Level 2: Increased radius (22 meters)
- Level 3: Silence duration increased (5 seconds)
- Level 4: Shield increased to 35% of nullified power
- Level 5: "Total Null" — Also removes passive abilities for 3 seconds

**Type:** Ultimate / Arena Transformation / Existential Threat

**Description:**
Void reveals a fraction of their true nature, transforming the battlefield into a preview of universal heat death. In this state, entropy accelerates, existence fades, and all things approach their inevitable end. This is not merely an attack — it is a demonstration of ultimate truth.

### Shader Requirements

```glsl
// Void event horizon shader
shader_type canvas_item;

uniform float gravity_strength : hint_range(0.0, 1.0) = 0.5;
uniform float desaturation : hint_range(0.0, 1.0) = 0.0;
uniform float time_dilation : hint_range(0.0, 1.0) = 0.0;
uniform sampler2D noise_texture;

void fragment() {
    vec2 uv = UV - vec2(0.5);
    
    // Gravitational lensing
    float dist = length(uv);
    float lensing = gravity_strength / (dist + 0.1);
    uv *= 1.0 - lensing * 0.1;
    uv += vec2(0.5);
    
    // Sample texture
    vec4 color = texture(TEXTURE, uv);
    
    // Desaturation
    float grey = dot(color.rgb, vec3(0.299, 0.587, 0.114));
    color.rgb = mix(color.rgb, vec3(grey), desaturation);
    
    // Time dilation effect (frozen particles)
    float noise = texture(noise_texture, UV + TIME * (1.0 - time_dilation)).r;
    if (time_dilation > 0.5 && noise < time_dilation * 0.3) {
        color.rgb *= 0.5; // Frozen effect
    }
    
    COLOR = color;
}
```

## Quest: "The Last Argument"

**Quest ID:** `quest_void_argument_01`

**Overview:**
Void offers the heroes a terrible choice: accept peaceful non-existence now, or struggle against the inevitable and suffer the greater tragedy later. The player must craft an argument for existence that even entropy cannot defeat.

**Act I — The Offer**
- Void appears not as enemy but as truth-teller
- Offers peaceful ending: "Why suffer? End now with dignity."
- Heroes tempted — existence IS temporary, struggle IS suffering

**Act II — The Crisis**
- Team debates Void's offer — some see wisdom in acceptance
- Player must gather evidence of existence's value
- Memories, connections, creations — what persists beyond death

**Act III — The Argument**
- Player presents case to Void
- Not denial of ending, but celebration of being
- **Key insight:** "The universe ends, yes. But we were here. That matters."

**Climax — The Paradox**
- Void cannot comprehend but cannot refute
- New possibility: Even eternal void may one day birth new existence
- Void retreats — not defeated, but... considering

**Rewards:**
- Title: "The Defier of Entropy"
- Lore unlock: Void's true origin
- Ability unlock: "Existence Affirmation" — temporary immunity to despair effects
- Secret: Void may become ally in final arc (the one thing that outlasts even them is meaning)
