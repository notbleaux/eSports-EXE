## TEAM FORMATION PRINCIPLES

### Why These Groupings?

1. **Task Proximity** — Agents working on related tasks
2. **Dependency Chains** — Upstream/downstream relationships
3. **Skill Complementarity** — Mixed expertise within team
4. **Pipeline Alignment** — Same pipeline for shared context
5. **Workload Balance** — Even distribution across TLs

### Team Leader Selection

**Criteria:**
- Technical depth in domain
- Communication clarity
- Decision-making comfort
- Empathy for team challenges
- Accountability mindset

**Process:**
1. Foreman identifies TL candidates from task assignments
2. Candidates briefed on TL responsibilities
3. Candidates accept or decline (no penalty)
4. Teams announced in roster
5. TL onboarding session with Foreman
