What is RAR?

Role-Adjusted Rating (RAR) normalizes player performance based on their role expectations. A Controller with 1.05 rating might have higher RAR than a Duelist with 1.10, because Controllers are expected to have lower raw stats.

A+ Elite - Top 5% within role
A Excellent - Top 15% within role
B+ Good - Above average for role
B Average - Meets role expectations
