## ⚖️ Why This Is Actually GOOD (Security)

### Imagine If I Could

If I could create accounts for you:
- I would need to know your email password
- I would create accounts in YOUR name
- I would have access to YOUR services
- You wouldn't know what I created

**This would be a massive security risk.**

### What Should Happen

You should create your own accounts because:
- ✅ You control your credentials
- ✅ You know what exists in your name
- ✅ You can revoke access
- ✅ No one else has your passwords
